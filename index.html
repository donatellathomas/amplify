About

This project explores the development of affordable instrument care and accessible music education. The brand aims to educate musicians about maintenance through clear visual language, straightforward instructional content, and cost-conscious solutions designed for musicians at every level.

By combining education with practical resources, the project emphasizes longevity, sustainability, and empowerment. Which in turn helps musicians better understand, care for, and connect with their instruments.
